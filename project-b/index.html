Make Chinese Intangible Cultural Heritage Alive

China's living heritage is not a relic of the past, but a dynamic, evolving, and deeply relevant part of our contemporary world.

"ICH Footsteps" is an interactive journey exploring China's Intangible Cultural Heritage through digital art. Players navigate a path discovering three traditional crafts: Iron Blossom that illuminate festivals with cascading sparks, Indigo Dyeing's flowing blue patterns that represent ancient textile mastery, and the Pentatonic Scale that transforms names into melodies using traditional Chinese five-note music theory. Each experience offers hands-on interaction, allowing users to create their own interpretations of these time-honored traditions. This project bridges ancient craftsmanship with modern technology, preserving cultural wisdom while making it accessible and engaging for contemporary audiences worldwide.
